# CodeBronx Packages

These packages give schools and organizations a clear way to work with CodeBronx. They are designed to move from awareness to implementation without overwhelming teachers or adding disconnected programming.

Pricing can be scoped based on timeline, audience size, number of sessions, materials needed, and whether support is virtual or in person.

---

## 1. Starter Workshop

**Best for:** schools or organizations exploring computational thinking, AI literacy, or computer science integration.

This is a strong entry point for teams that need shared language, practical examples, and a clear next step.

### Includes

- One 60- to 90-minute professional development session
- Practical examples connected to ELA, Math, AI literacy, or STEM
- Teacher-facing planning template
- Resource packet
- Optional leadership debrief

### Strong Fit For

- Principal cabinet meetings
- Teacher professional learning days
- Introductory AI literacy sessions
- School teams exploring CS/CT integration
- CBOs or youth programs seeking a practical computing education entry point

### Possible Outcomes

Participants leave with:

- A clearer understanding of computational thinking or AI literacy
- Examples they can connect to current instruction
- A practical planning tool
- A next-step recommendation for implementation

---

## 2. Implementation Sprint

**Best for:** schools ready to test computing education in real classrooms.

This package moves beyond a single workshop and supports teachers as they plan, try, and refine classroom-ready activities.

### Includes

- 2–4 professional learning sessions
- Curriculum review or lesson selection support
- Teacher planning templates
- Classroom-ready lesson extensions
- Optional model lesson or co-planning session
- Implementation reflection tool
- Final recommendations summary

### Strong Fit For

- Grade teams
- ELA or Math departments
- Schools piloting AI literacy routines
- Schools integrating computational thinking into existing instruction
- Teams that want evidence before scaling schoolwide

### Possible Outcomes

Schools leave with:

- A small set of usable lesson extensions
- Teachers who understand how CT or AI literacy connects to instruction
- Evidence from implementation
- A clearer plan for scaling the work

---

## 3. Schoolwide Partnership

**Best for:** schools or districts building a sustainable computing education system.

This package supports planning, professional development, coaching, implementation, and refinement across a longer timeline.

### Includes

- Leadership planning session
- Curriculum and instructional priority review
- Multi-session professional development series
- Coaching or office hours
- Model lesson support
- Teacher-facing materials
- Student-facing tools
- Implementation evidence collection
- Final impact summary and next-step roadmap

### Strong Fit For

- Schools building an AI literacy plan
- Schools integrating computational thinking across multiple grades
- Districts or networks supporting CS/AI implementation
- Organizations building STEM or workforce-connected learning programs
- Schools that want the work to continue beyond one enthusiastic teacher

### Possible Outcomes

Schools can build:

- A shared instructional vision for computing education
- Teacher-ready resources
- Repeatable planning routines
- Student artifacts connected to real learning
- A stronger foundation for AI literacy, CS, STEM, and career-connected learning

---

## 4. Custom Curriculum or Resource Design

**Best for:** organizations, schools, or programs that need specific materials built.

This is for teams that already know what they need but need help designing high-quality, usable resources.

### Possible Deliverables

- Lesson plans
- Slide decks
- Student handouts
- Teacher guides
- Curriculum maps
- PD facilitator guides
- AI literacy workflows
- Computational thinking activities
- Robotics or physical computing challenges
- Implementation toolkits

### Strong Fit For

- Grant-funded projects
- District initiatives
- Youth STEM programs
- School-based pilots
- Professional development programs
- Organizations building reusable instructional assets

## How to Choose the Right Package

| If you need... | Start with... |
|---|---|
| A clear introduction for staff | Starter Workshop |
| Teachers to try the work in classrooms | Implementation Sprint |
| A schoolwide or districtwide system | Schoolwide Partnership |
| Materials, lessons, or tools built for your program | Custom Curriculum or Resource Design |

- Sustainable beyond one workshop
