Digital Storytelling with Scratch

Helping students animate written narratives through code.

Workshop Goals

  • Bridge CS and ELA through narrative structure
  • Empower students to tell stories through Scratch
  • Model decomposition and sequencing for teachers

Structure of the 3-Day PD

  1. Day 1: Decomposition + Storyboarding
  2. Day 2: Scratch Code-Along & Scene Building
  3. Day 3: Extensions, SEL Integration, Planning

Facilitation Tools

Scratch, Google Slides, Padlet, Wakelet, Bit.ly, Zoom

Participant Outcomes

  • Each teacher built a working Scratch prototype
  • Implementation plan for classroom rollout
  • Positive feedback on modeling + planning tools
