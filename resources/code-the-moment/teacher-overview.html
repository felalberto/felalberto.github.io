Teacher Overview

Use this overview to understand the project before introducing it to students.

CodeBronx
Code the Moment

What students will make

Students choose one small moment from a story and turn it into a short Scratch animation that shows the story through characters, setting, dialogue, movement, sequence, and debugging.

Best classroom fit

Use this resource when students are working with story moments, character decisions, or narrative structure.
