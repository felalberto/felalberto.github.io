1. Choose

Students select a story.

2. Focus

Students choose one small moment.

3. Decompose

Students break the scene into parts.

4. Plan

Students map story parts to Scratch.

5. Build

Students create the scene.

6. Debug + Share

Students test, revise, and explain.
