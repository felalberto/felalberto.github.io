Adaptation Guide

Connect the project to different ELA goals and Scratch skill levels.

CodeBronx
Adaptations
ELA FocusStudent TaskTeacher Question
Character TraitsAnimate a moment that shows what a character is like.What does the character say or do that reveals their trait?
SequenceAnimate one important scene in order:
  1. beginning
  2. middle
  3. end
What has to happen in order for this moment to make sense?
DialogueUse say blocks to show what characters say or think.How does the dialogue help the audience understand the scene?
ThemeAnimate a moment that helps reveal the message.How does this scene connect to the lesson or message?
Point of ViewAnimate the same moment from one character's perspective.How would this scene look or sound from this character's view?
Beginner:
  • sprites
  • backdrops
  • say blocks
  • green flag events
Developing:
  • movement
  • timing
  • costume changes
  • clearer sequence
Intermediate:
  • loops
  • sounds
  • multiple sprites
  • simple scene changes
Advanced:
  • multiple backdrops
  • broadcasts
  • variables
  • choices or interactive elements
