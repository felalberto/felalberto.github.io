Student Project Sheet

Turn one small moment from a story into a Scratch animation.

Name: ___________
Date: ___________

Your challenge

Choose one important or interesting moment from a story. Then use Scratch to bring that moment to life with characters, setting, dialogue, movement, and sequence.

Steps

  1. Choose a story or use the story your teacher gives you.
  2. Pick one small moment from the story.
  3. Identify the characters, setting, actions, and dialogue.
  4. Break the moment into smaller steps.
  5. Plan how each step will look in Scratch.
  6. Build, test, debug, and share.

Success criteria

  • I chose one focused story moment.
  • I included characters and setting.
  • I used dialogue or narration.
  • I showed action or movement.
  • My scene happens in a clear order.
  • I tested and debugged my project.
